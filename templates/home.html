The University of Dodoma

The University of Dodoma (UDOM) is a public university located in the city of Dodoma, the capital of Tanzania. It is located at the center of the Country at the Capital city, Dodoma. It was established in 2007 with the goal of becoming a leading research and teaching institution in the country. UDOM offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as education, engineering, law, medicine, and the social sciences. The university is organized into eight faculties, including the Faculty of Education, the Faculty of Engineering and Technology, and the Faculty of Law. UDOM is committed to providing high-quality education and conducting cutting-edge research to address the challenges facing Tanzania and the broader African continent.
